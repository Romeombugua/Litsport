Why Litigation Funding in Sports?

Litigation funding is consistently outperforming other investments like gold, bonds, and real estate. Hedge fund heavyweights, venture capitalists, and elite investors have recognized its potential for high returns—and now, with Litsport, we've made it possible for anyone to invest.
